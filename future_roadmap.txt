POTENTIAL FUTURE FEATURES FOR STUDOR PATHCREDIT LOGGER

Here are some cool ideas to make this app even better in the future, explained in simple terms:

1. Log In Feature
Right now, your data is only on this one browser. In the future, we could add a "Log In" so you can see your activities from your phone, laptop, or any other device.

2. Progress Charts
We could add simple graphs or pie charts that show you which categories (like Academic or Technical) you are spending the most time on. It's a great way to see your growth with pictures!

3. Saving to the Internet (Cloud)
Instead of just saving files on your computer, we can save them on the internet. This means you can upload as many heavy photos or certificates as you want without worrying about space.

4. Download as a Report
Imagine a button that turns your entire feed into a beautiful PDF file. You could use this report to show your teachers or even put it in your resume!

5. Dark Mode
A "Night Mode" toggle would make the screen dark and easy on the eyes when you're logging activities late at night.

6. Search Bar
If you have 100 activities, finding one can be hard. A search bar would let you type a name (like "React") and find it instantly.

7. Setting Goals
You could set a goal, like "Complete 5 Technical Activities this month," and the app would show you a progress bar as you get closer to finishing it.

8. Share with Friends
A simple button to share your certificates or milestones directly to LinkedIn, WhatsApp, or through email.

9. Better Organization (Tags)
Besides just categories, you could add custom tags like "#Workshop" or "#Competition" to group your activities even better.
